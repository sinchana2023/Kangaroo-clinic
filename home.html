Know Your Doctor

Dr Poonam is a pediatrician in Basvangudi.She has 20yrs experience in this field. During her tenure she's managed General Pediatric wards, PICU , Special clinics like Asthma clinic , Adolescent Obesity clinics. She started Adolescent subspecialty in St Martha's.
She was one of teaching faculty for DNB programme in St.Marthas and Co-guide to PG Research and Thesis.
She is also a committee member for Infectious Diseases conference and National Adolescent conferences. At present she's practising at Kangroo Clinic, Basvangudi, Banglore.
Apart from being a member of the Indian pediatric association, Karnataka medical council, Dr. Poonam is committed towards providing the best healthcare treatment for children. Pediatricians are concerned with health of infants, children and teenagers. Dr. Poonam's approach lies in performing diagnostic tests to obtain information about patients medical condition and administer therapies, medications and vaccinations to treat illness, disorders and injuries.
A part of being a pediatrician is not only dealing with the children but also dealing with the family. A typical day of Dr. Poonam starts by visiting the new born nursery, addressing concerns of the parents and moving further to the pediatric care
